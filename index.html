Literary Ladies Book Club

Welcome to the Literary Ladies Book Club website! We’re a small group of book lovers who love to share good books and yummy treats. We work hard to make sure that the books that we choose to read don’t contain excessive vulgar language, violence, or explicit scenes. We meet together once a month for a couple hours, usually on a Tuesday evening, and discuss our latest book choice. We always end with a delicious treat that one of our members has prepared.

If you'd like more information on our book club or are interested in joining us, please click on the button below to send us a message!
